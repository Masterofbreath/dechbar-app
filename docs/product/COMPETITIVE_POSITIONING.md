## Executive Summary

DechBar enters the Czech wellness market as the **first application dedicated exclusively to functional breathing**. While competitors focus on meditation (Calmio), mental health therapy (VOS.health), or lifestyle influencing (5 Pilířů Zdraví), DechBar creates an entirely new category: **"Respiratory Optimization Technology"**.

**Core Positioning:**
```
"První česká aplikace pro funkční dýchání s měřitelnými výsledky"
```

**Unique Value Proposition:**
- ONLY app measuring breathing capacity (BOLT/KP score)
- Science-first approach (Bohr effect, nitric oxide, CO2 tolerance)
- Czech certification + local authority
- Data-driven optimization (not just relaxation)

## Detailed Competitor Analysis

### Competitor #1: Calmio

**Website:** https://www.calmio.cz  
**Positioning:** "První česká meditační aplikace podložená vědou"

**Strengths:**
- Strong scientific positioning ("podložená vědou")
- Czech-first branding
- Professional design
- Established user base
- Corporate wellness programs

**Weaknesses:**
- Generic meditation (not unique methodology)
- No objective progress measurement
- Broad positioning (anxiety, sleep, stress - everything)
- Subscription fatigue (monthly payment model)

**Our Advantage:**
- **Category difference:** We're NOT meditation, we're breathing optimization
- **Measurement:** We track BOLT score objectively, they track mood subjectively
- **Specificity:** Narrow focus on breathing = deeper expertise
- **Pricing:** Lifetime options vs. endless subscription

**How to position against:**
```
"Calmio tě uklidní. DechBar tě změní."
"Oni meditují. My měříme."
```

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk #1: "Another wellness app" perception

**Mitigation:**
- Lead with measurement (BOLT score) everywhere
- Science-first messaging (Bohr effect visible)
- Avoid generic wellness language
- Show data, not just claims

### Risk #2: "Ezo" association

**Mitigation:**
- Never use blacklisted words (energie, harmonie, čakry)
- Professional endorsements (MUDr., not influencers)
- Research references visible
- Tone: Technical + friendly, not spiritual

### Risk #3: Too niche (only breathing)

**Mitigation:**
- Frame as "foundation of health" (breathing affects everything)
- Show broad benefits (sleep, stress, energy, performance)
- Not "just breathing" - "respiratory optimization"

### Risk #4: Price sensitivity (Czech market)

**Mitigation:**
- Strong ZDARMA tier (build trust)
- Lifetime options (no subscription fatigue)
- Frame as "investment in health" not "cost"
- Compare to coffee: "Méně než jedna káva týdně"
